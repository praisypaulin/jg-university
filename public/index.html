Who We Are

Shaping Global
Change Makers

JG University is a tech-driven institution in the heart of Ahmedabad, Gujarat. Sponsored by the ASIA Charitable Trust — active in education since 1965 — we evolve our programs to match the demands of the future workforce.

Our pedagogy fuses academic rigor with real-world exposure: industry partnerships, live projects, paid internships, and mentorship from domain experts who bridge theory and practice.
